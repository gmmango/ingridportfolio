Project Overview

Soul of Vitória-Régia U+002d a stylized 3D character and environment inspired by Brazilian folklore. Responsible for concept, sculpting, retopology, texturing, shading, and rendering. Includes character breakdown, clay renders, and final environment shots.

Creation Process

This project began with concept sketches and asset blocking in Blender. Rigging and animation were handled with precision to ensure fluid motion. Texturing was done using Substance Painter, followed by final lighting and rendering in the primary renderer.

The compositing stage brought all elements together, enhancing depth and atmosphere. Special attention was paid to detail and storytelling through movement and environment.
