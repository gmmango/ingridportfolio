Project Overview

A high-energy VFX sequence featuring destruction, particle effects, and advanced compositing techniques. Demonstrates mastery of simulation software and post-production workflows.

Creation Process

This project began with concept sketches and asset blocking in Houdini. Rigging and animation were handled with precision to ensure fluid motion. Texturing was done using Maya, followed by final lighting and rendering in the primary renderer.

The compositing stage brought all elements together, enhancing depth and atmosphere. Special attention was paid to detail and storytelling through movement and environment.
