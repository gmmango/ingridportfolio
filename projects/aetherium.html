Project Overview

A futuristic motion graphics piece showcasing energy flow and particle systems around a central core. Features complex dynamics, procedural animations, and striking visual effects.

Creation Process

This project began with concept sketches and asset blocking in Cinema 4D. Rigging and animation were handled with precision to ensure fluid motion. Texturing was done using After Effects, followed by final lighting and rendering in the primary renderer.

The compositing stage brought all elements together, enhancing depth and atmosphere. Special attention was paid to detail and storytelling through movement and environment.
